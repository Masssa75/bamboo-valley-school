Cherngtalay's Nature School

Bamboo Valley is a kindergarten in Cherngtalay offering something rare: a 3.5-rai palm plantation where children spend their days learning outdoors, caring for animals, and developing naturally.

Centrally located in Cherngtalay village, we serve families from Bangtao, Laguna, Surin, and the surrounding areas. Our campus is a 5-minute drive from Bangtao Beach and 10 minutes from Laguna Phuket.
