Behavior Support

Clear rules. Fair consequences. A system that protects everyone while helping children learn.

Our approach: When a child breaks a rule, we respond with calm, consistent consequences â€” and then we work to understand why it happened so we can prevent it next time. Consequences without understanding doesn't teach. Understanding without consequences doesn't protect.

The 4 Rules

These rules apply to all children, all the time, in all settings. We teach them through stories, role-play, and daily practice.

1
Gentle Hands
No pushing, hitting, or grabbing
2
Kind Voice
No yelling, mean words, or teasing
3
Share & Wait
Take turns, practice patience
4
Everyone Plays
"You can't play" is not allowed

If Serious Incidents Continue

We believe in giving children chances to learn and grow. But we also have a responsibility to protect all children. Here's what happens when serious incidents continue:

1
1st serious incident
Parent meeting. Behavior contract introduced.
2

Behavior contract signed. Formal monitoring begins.
3

Child sent home for the day. Meeting required before return.
4
4th serious incident
Suspension (1-3 days). Enrollment review.

Beyond Consequences

Consequences address the moment. But they don't solve the underlying problem. That's why we also work to understand why a child is struggling and help them develop the skills they need.
