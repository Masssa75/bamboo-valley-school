My Recommendations

For maximum clarity: Option 4 (Pill) or Option 7 (Outlined) - clearly a clickable element

For clean/minimal design: Option 3 (Globe) or Option 5 (Code Only) - professional, no emoji issues

For space efficiency: Option 2 (Flag Only) - minimal footprint

Current implementation is good: Option 1 is clear and recognizable, just consider if the flag emojis render well on all devices
